New York style Scramble Egg Sandwich

Description

What actually makes something "New York style" I have no idea. Since my aunt was the first person to introduce me to these sandwiches and since she lives in New York, I guess this is why I consider it to be New York Style. Then again this recipe isn't any different from any other scrambled eggs and toast. Oh well my webpage, my recipe.

What are you waiting for, go on and try this recipe for yourself! It should takes less than 10 mins. And it is perfect to eat in the mornings,lunch or before taking a four hour bus ride home.

Ingrediants

  • 1 Egg
  • 2 slices of Toast
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Crack open an egg into a small bowl and add some salt
  2. Wisked virogusly with a fork
  3. Add a tiny bit of water to the bowl
  4. Pour a tiny bit of oil into a pan and put the tempature to medium
  5. When the oil is hot pour the egg bowl into the pan
  6. Using your spatula keep mixing the egg around until it is soft
  7. Once soften turn off the heat and put your bread into the toaster
  8. After the bread is toasted place the two slices on a plate
  9. Now put your scrambled eggs on top of the toast. Sprinkle with pepper or parsely as desired
  10. Enjoy!
