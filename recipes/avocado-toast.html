Avocado Toast

Description

Avocado Toast is a meal that can be enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. It is a very simple recipe that only uses minimal ingrediants. While many people would pay $10 to $15 for a slice of Avocado Toast at a resturant, you can easily recreate the same meal at home for as little as $3.

This simple reciepe can easily be modify to your liking. Feel free to add your own unique twist such as adding a hard boiled or poached egg. Adding some greens like spinch or kale. Or topping it off with some nice reds like tomatoes or red pepper.

Ingrediants

  • 1 Avocado
  • 2 Pieces of Bread
  • Black pepper
  • Lime
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash and slice the avocado in half
  2. Discard the pitt, and using a spoon scoop out the avocado into a bowl
  3. Using a fork begin smashing the avocado. Feel free to add as much salt and lime as desired
  4. Keep smashing the avocado until the consistentcy is smooth
  5. Lightly toast the bread in the toaster
  6. Using a knife spread the avocado paste on the two slices of bread
  7. Sprinkle some pepper
  8. Enjoy!
