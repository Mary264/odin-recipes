Peanut Butter and Jelly (PB and J)

Description

An American childhood favorite, Peanut Butter and Jelly or PB and J sandwich is sandwich you will not be able to stop eating. Who knew that a sandich just made up of two ingrediants would be beloved by so many. Even as a young preschooler when asked what did I love the most about my mom, I said it was her pb and j sandwiches.

Peanut butter and Jelly sandwiches are so famous that they even have their own song "It's peanut butter jelly time." And when I was abroad in England my friends requested that I make them pb and j sandwiches. While some people may think they are saving money by buying peanut butter and jelly already mixed in a jar the quality is terrible. You don't want to be cheep on this reciepe

Also it is argued that pb and j sandwiches cut diagonally taste much better!

Ingrediants

  • Peanut butter (smooth or crunchy)
  • Grape Jelly
  • 2 pieces of Bread

Steps

  1. Toast your bread
  2. Grab your peanut butter and spread it on one slice
  3. On the other slice of bread spread the jelly
  4. Put the two slices together
  5. Cut the sandwich in half diagonally
  6. Enjoy!
